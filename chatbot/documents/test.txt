Our company leave policy allows employees 18 days of annual leave per year.
Employees must apply for leave at least 7 days in advance.
Sick leave is separate and allows up to 10 days per year with a medical certificate.
Maternity leave is 26 weeks fully paid.
Paternity leave is 2 weeks fully paid.
All leave requests must be approved by the direct manager.
Unused annual leave can be carried forward to the next year up to a maximum of 10 days.
Work from home is allowed up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
